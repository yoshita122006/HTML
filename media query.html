MOVIE REVIEW

Surya S/O Krishnan

Movie title:Surya S/O Krishnan

Director:Gautham Vasudev Menon

Year of release:2008

Genre:Action/Romance

Duration:2h 43m

Cast:Suriya - as Krishnan and Surya
Sameera Reddy - as Meghna
Divya Spandana (Ramya) - as Priya
Simran - as Malini

Music director:Harris Jayaraj

Distributed by:Cloud Nine Movies

Surya S/O Krishnan is one of those films that stays with you long after it ends. It takes you through Surya's life — his dreams, love, and struggles — in such a heartfelt way. Suriya's performance feels so real, and the music byHarris Jayaraj just makes every scene more beautiful. It's emotional, relatable, and worth watching
